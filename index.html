Tetra-Block Game Background

About:

The Tetra-Block Game Background is a game that you can easily insert into an existing website, or build a brand new website around it. The game randomly generates falling blocks that can be moved around by your visitors with the arrow keys. This will help prevent your visitors from getting bored with your website and dramatically decreases the bounce rate. Great for creating an impressive portfolio!

  • Easy to place in ANY existing website
  • Randomly generated puzzle game
  • Quietly runs in the background of your site
  • Keeps visitors in your site
  • Spruces up your portfolio
